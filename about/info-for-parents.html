Information for Parents

Age ratings

Gaming consoles usually have age ratings that indicate the recommended minimum age for playing a particular game. It's essential for parents to check these ratings to ensure the game is appropriate for their child's age.

Parental controls

Most gaming consoles offer parental control features that allow parents to set restrictions on things like buying games, accessing online content, and limiting screen time. Make sure you familiarize yourself with these features and utilize them to create a safe gaming environment for your child.

Online safety

Many modern gaming consoles have online capabilities, which means your child may interact with other players over the internet. Teach your child about online safety, such as not sharing personal information, being cautious when chatting with strangers, and reporting any inappropriate behavior.

Monitoring gameplay

Take an interest in your child's gaming habits by monitoring their gameplay.
